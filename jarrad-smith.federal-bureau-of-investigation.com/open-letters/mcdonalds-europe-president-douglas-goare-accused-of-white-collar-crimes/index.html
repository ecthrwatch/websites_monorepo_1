Even though I’ve never contacted you in person before, I believe it’s impossible for you not to be aware that I am accusing McDonald’s of having committed multiple massive frauds in different countries within the European Union.

Therefore, I am accusing you of being a white-collar criminal.

I’ve created the website white-collar-criminals.com and you will be listed on it from now on.

I am offering you a right of reply but you must use it within 48 hours.

The criminal complaints which have been filed don’t only target McDonald’s corporation. They also target individual executives like Mr. Easterbrook, Ms. Santona (who is additionally suspected of insider trading), Mr. Petit, Mr. Hicks etc.

The criminal complaint has also been filed against the French law firm Péchenard which has taken part in the fraud. The huissier de justice (a French official) is also targeted by the criminal complaint since he knew about the fraud since at least 2012.

Also, I am ordering you to cease immediately the ongoing fraud which is happening in Germany as of right now and which is in direct violation of the European directive 2005/29/CE.

Best regards,
